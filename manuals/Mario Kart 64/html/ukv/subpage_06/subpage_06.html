Modes
MARIO GP
Race across four courses to compete for the best overall rank. Each course consists of three laps. Choose your engine size, character, and cup to get started.
VS
Race three laps on a single course. The first player to reach the goal wins! Choose engine size, characters, and a course to begin.
BATTLE
Attack rivals to pop their balloons. The first player to pop all the other drivers' balloons wins. Choose characters and a course to begin.
T.TRIALS
Drive as fast as you can on a single course to try and set a course record. If you keep racing on the same course, you can race against the course ghost*. Once you choose a new course, the ghost will vanish. Choose a character and course to begin racing.

*Because the Virtual Console does not use a Controller Pak, ghost data cannot be saved.
