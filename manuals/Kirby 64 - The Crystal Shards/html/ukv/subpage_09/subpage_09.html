Bonus Games

At the end of each stage, you will get a chance to play a Bonus Game. Make Kirby jump to collect the items.

Kirby turns from left to right. Press when he is facing the item you want to get.

Kirby swings his arms forward and backward. Press to jump. When his arms are swung backward, he will jump a long way. When they are forward, he will jump a shorter distance. Consider the distance to the item you want and time your press of well.
