Mini-games

There are three mini-games available under Mini-games in the Options menu. Choose a game to play, the number of players, the characters to use, and the COM (computer) players' levels before starting the game.

All mini-games are played by four players. If there are fewer than four human players participating, the computer will take control of the remaining players. Only the EASY level is available at first. You must win mini-games to unlock the more difficult levels.
