Actions (2)
Sword Actions
Swinging Vertically Press and hold , then press to swing your sword in a downward arc.
Swinging Horizontally Press , or press and hold , then tilt left or right while pressing to swing your sword from side to side.
Swinging Consecutively You can swing your sword three times in a row. Press at the right moment to make your third swing much wider.
Stabbing Press and hold , then tilt up and press to stab in front of you.
Spin Swinging Press and hold for a short time, then release it to perform a spin swing. If you've learned magic, you can use small or large spin swings depending on how long you hold down .
Other Actions
Rolling Attack Press while running to do a roll attack. Attacks that strike you while rolling will be reflected as if you had blocked them with your shield.
Jumping Attack While holding your sword, press and hold , then press to leap in the air and swing your sword with twice the power of a standard swing.
Side Jumping Press and hold , then tilt left or right while pressing to jump sideways.
Performing a Backflip Press and hold , then tilt down while pressing to do a backflip.
Raising Your Shield Press to raise your shield. Use to move it.
