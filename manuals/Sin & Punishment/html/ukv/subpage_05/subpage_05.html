Basic Play

Advance through each stage and take out any enemy that stands in your way. Defeat the boss at the end of each stage to clear the area and move on to the next stage.

Damage and Game Over
Each time you get hit by an enemy attack or fall a great distance, you will lose life from your Life Gauge.

Also, if you run out of time, your Life Gauge will begin to gradually deplete. If your Life Gauge empties, your game will end, and you'll be taken to the results screen.

Ranking
At the results screen, if you scored well enough in your game, you will be given the opportunity to enter your initials and record your score. Choose letters with , and press to input letters.

Credits and Continuing
After you've finished on the results screen, you will be taken to the continue screen. Once there, choose between either Continue or Save and Quit.

You must have at least one credit to continue your game. You are awarded one credit for every 100 enemies you defeat.
