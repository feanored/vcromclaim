Controls



*You can change your control scheme under the Controls option in the main menu. The control scheme explained here assumes you are using Type 1 controls.
Moving Around
Move Left or Right Press left with or to move to the left, and press right with or to move to the right.
Roll Quickly press right with or twice to roll to the right. Quickly press left with or twice to roll to the left. You will not take damage when rolling.

Attacking
Change How You Target Press to switch between Manual Type targeting, where you must line your cursor up manually to target an enemy, and Lock-On Type targeting, where your cursor will automatically lock on to enemies that are in the direction you press the and . When using Lock-On Type targeting, the power of your shots is decreased.
