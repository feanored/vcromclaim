Saving

Each time you successfully complete a stage, the number of stages you can select from the Scene Select option will increase by one. You can save your progress in one of the two following ways.

Saving at the Pause Menu
Select the Save and Quit option on the menu to save and exit out of your current game. However, be aware that when you load up your game next, you will start from a designated point within the stage that may not correspond to the exact point from which you left off.

Saving at the Continue Screen
You can also save your game at the continue screen by selecting the Save and Quit option there.

Note: When you have no credits remaining and die it's game over: you must start over from the beginning of the game. When you choose to erase your save data using the Save Data option on the main menu, your ranking information and stages unlocked in the Scene Select option are erased as well.
About Saving
 When saving your game, please be aware that any of the following actions may cause you to lose your save data. If you lose your save data in any of these ways, there is no way to recover it, so please be careful.
• While data is being saved, pressing RESET or turning off the power on either the Wii console or Wii Remote.
• While data is being saved, selecting either the Wii Menu or Reset options from the HOME Menu.
• Disconnecting the Wii AC Adapter from your Wii console while it is turned on.
