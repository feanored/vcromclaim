Items

Notes		 Collecting 100 will give you an extra life.

Magic Suit		 If you collect one, your suit will turn blue, and it will protect you from touching or getting hit by an enemy once. Collecting two Magic Suits will give you a Dream Suit that will turn your suit green and protect you twice.

Treble Bubble		 Lets you blow up to three bubbles in a row.

Bubble Distance		 Increases the distance of your bubbles.

Bowling Ball		 Roll this ball to knock over enemies.

Balloon Gum		 Saves you once when you fall into a pit.

Milon		 Gives you an extra life.

Miracle Star		 Makes you invincible for a short time.

Floaty Shoes		 Pressing while in midair will let you fall slowly.

Musical Star		 Collecting five of these will release the curse on a Magical Instrument.

Castle Key		 Unlocks the door to the boss area in each level.

Crystal		 Allows you to enter Amon's castle.

Spike Shoes		 Help you to walk safely on ice. If you get stuck in the ice level, go to the first area of that level and talk to the couple in the hut in order to obtain these shoes.
