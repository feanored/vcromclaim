Basic Play

One day, the demon Amon swooped down on the village of Piccolo, stole the Magical Instruments, and kidnapped the fairy Aeris. Her friend Milon decided to set off on a journey to rescue her.

As Milon, you must clear 7 levels full of monsters to get to Amon. Each level is made up of a number of areas. Hidden in the last area of each level is a Castle Key that you will need to get to the boss area. Defeating the boss will take you to the next level. After defeating certain bosses, you will get Magical Instruments that will give you special abilities to progress through the game.

From level 2 on, you will need to find the five Musical Stars hidden in each level in order to release the curse on a Magical Instrument and fight the boss of that level. When a blinking star appears on an area of the map screen, it means that there is a Musical Star hidden in that area. Once you have found it, the blinking star will disappear. You can check the number of Musical Stars you have collected on the status screen.

Attack Methods




Stomp Attack
Jumping on top of an enemy will knock it out for a little while. Enemies are harmless when they're knocked out, so you can use them as platforms to get to higher places.

Bubble & Push
Hitting an enemy with a bubble will trap it in the bubble for a few seconds. Walk into the bubble before it bursts, and you'll send it flying off the screen!
