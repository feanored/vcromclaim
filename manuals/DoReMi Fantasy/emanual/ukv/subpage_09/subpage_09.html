Mini Games

Entering some doors and caves will take you to one of several mini games. Your score will determine the items and other goodies you’ll win at the end of the game.

Bird Counting
Birds will fly from one side of the screen to the other. Some of them will stop and go back the way they came. Count only the number of birds that cross the screen. Select the number by pressing left or right on , and press to confirm. If you are right, you’ll play another level of this mini game; if you are wrong, the mini game will end.

Toy Bird Hunt
Toy birds will fly from the left side of the screen. Use to move the target, and press to shoot. The points obtained will be displayed in the bottom-right corner of the screen. The number of toy birds remaining will be displayed in the top-right corner of the screen.

What Just Flew By?
Different items will cross the screen, and you will have to remember them in the correct order in which they appear. Select the items with , and press to confirm. You will have a limited time to do it, so try to be as fast as you can!
