Controls


*While playing a course, you can press , , , and / simultaneously to return to the Yoshi Select screen. This will cost you one life.

*, , , and have the same function.

Crouch Tilt down to crouch.
Enter a Pipe/Vase Stand on an upward-facing pipe or vase, then tilt down to enter. You can also stand below a downward-facing pipe, jump, and continue tilting up to enter from below.
Enter a Door Stand in front of a door and tilt up to enter.
Flutter Jump Press and hold while in the air to float for a short time.
Ground Pound Tilt down while in the air to perform a ground pound.
Sniff-Sniff Sniff with to search for hidden objects. If you do a ground pound at the point where Yoshi has the strongest reaction, you might find something good!
Throw an Egg Press and hold , then adjust your aim with . Release when you are ready to fire. You can cancel a throw by pressing while aiming.
Launch as an Egg While Yoshi is inside an egg, you can tilt and release to fly in the opposite direction until the egg breaks.
Return to Normal from inside Egg While Yoshi is inside an egg, press to return to normal.
