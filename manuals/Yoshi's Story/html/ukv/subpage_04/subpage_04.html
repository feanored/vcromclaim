Getting Started

Press on the title screen to access the mode-select screen. Select a mode with and press to confirm.

If you want to learn Yoshi's moves, head to Practice first.

Story Mode Play along with a story. The story has six pages of four courses each. Choose a course and a Yoshi to get started.

*Depending on how many Special Hearts you have found, the courses you can choose on the next page may be different.
Trial Mode Play courses you have cleared in Story Mode. While playing, you can press and on the pause screen to return to the course-select screen.
Options Configure settings and erase data here.
Practice Take a spin through a practice course where you can see explanations for controlling Yoshi and using items. Press at any time to return to the mode-select screen.
