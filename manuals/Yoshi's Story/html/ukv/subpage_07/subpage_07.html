Basic Play

Control Yoshi as he advances through the course. You can swallow enemies and turn them into eggs, jump on them, or use a ground pound or egg throw to defeat them. Swallow 30 pieces of fruit to clear a course and advance to the next page. Clear the final course to win the game.

Smile Meter Petals
Your Smile Meter petals increase when you swallow fruit or enemies, and decrease when you take damage by touching an enemy or swallowing a bad-tasting enemy.

Lives and Game Over
If you lose all of your petals then take damage, you will lose a life. If you lose all of your lives, your game will end.
