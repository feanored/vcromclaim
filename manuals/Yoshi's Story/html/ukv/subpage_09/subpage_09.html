Characters


Shy Guy		 Do a ground pound to change his color.

White Shy Guy		 These friendly Shy Guys will follow Yoshi. If they are around when you clear a course, maybe something good will happen...

Miss Warp		 There are four of these on each course. Hop on her head to wake her up, then jump off to warp to the next awake Miss Warp in numerical order (1 > 2 > 3 > 4 > 1). If you lose a life, you will restart from the Miss Warp with the highest number.

Pak E. Derm		 Ground-pound near him to knock him over so you can pass.

Poochy		 This cute little guy reacts to areas where items are hidden.
